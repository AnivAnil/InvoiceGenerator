🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

1. Data Storage

POS Retail Pro stores all your data locally in your browser using localStorage. We do not have any servers, databases, or cloud storage. Your information never leaves your device.

2. What Data Is Stored

  • Your account details (name, username, email, hashed‑like password).
  • Shop information (store name, address, contact, logo).
  • Invoices (customer details, items, totals, notes).
  • Admin approval records.

3. Third‑Party Access

We do not share, sell, or have access to your data. Since everything stays on your device, no third party can ever obtain it through our application.

4. Data Export & Import

You can export your full account as a JSON file. This file contains all your data. Keep it safe. Anyone with this file can restore your account on another device.

5. Security

Your password is stored in plain text within localStorage (similar to a local file). This is suitable for a local‑only application. We recommend using a strong, unique password and not sharing your device.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact the admin at anilsouth65@gmail.com.
