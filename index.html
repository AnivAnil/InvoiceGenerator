FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about POS Retail Pro.

Is POS Retail Pro really free? +

Yes, absolutely! POS Retail Pro is 100% free with all features included. There are no hidden fees, premium tiers, or payment walls. We built this for Indian retailers who need a simple, powerful billing tool.

How does admin approval work? +

When a new user registers, their account is marked as "pending." The admin (you or a manager) reviews the request and clicks "Approve" or "Reject" from the admin dashboard. Only approved users can log in and create invoices.

Can I use it on multiple devices? +

Yes! Use the Export button on your dashboard to download your full account data. Then on any other device, use the "Restore from Backup" option on the login page to import everything — your account, invoices, and settings.

Where is my data stored? +

All data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. There is no server, no cloud storage, and no third‑party access. Your business data stays completely private and under your control at all times.

Can I customize the GST percentages? +

Absolutely. You set CGST, SGST, and Cess percentages manually for every invoice. This gives you complete flexibility to handle different product categories and tax rates as per Indian GST regulations.
